About Me

“Choose a job you love, and you will never have to work a day in your life…” - Confucius

As a UX/UI and Web Designer, you could say, the internet is my place of inspiration, and my devices are my art canvas.

I possess a constant thirst for perfecting my skills as a designer, so that I can create uniquely-intuitive webpage and mobile app designs fit for anyone to enjoy. Coupled with that, my growing knowledge of Front-End Development allow me to fully bring my work to life, to share with others in my place of inspiration both in desktop and mobile form.

I love what I do, and I look forward to showing you this same passion by offering my skills to you.
