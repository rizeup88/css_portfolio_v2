BIG3 Fanatic

Basketball Fan Webpage

BIG3 is a different kind of professional basketball league fans can enjoy. Created by rapper and actor Ice Cube in 2017, this league brings together both newcomer players, and legends of the NBA, and provides a unique format that gives fans something fun to enjoy all summer long.

During the maiden voyage of the league, both myself and a creative partner decided to create a special webpage dedicated to extending the fanfare of BIG3 even further. Using WordPress-based webpage tools, combined with creating unique branding tied to the league, we set up a one-stop resource for fans to keep updated on news, schedules, and media available directly from the league.

As the league enters it's next season, the webpage has garnered enthusiastic praise from members of the league, including legendary coach Julius "Dr. J" Erving, and current Tri-State forward Jermaine O'Neal.
