cart-blánche

Food & Drink Mobile App

In today’s hectic lifestyle, finding a spot to eat at on the go is much easier with the advent of food-carts. But with so many different cuisines out there, and plenty of choices available for quick meals, how do you search for one closer to you in a more organized and streamlined way?

This was my goal when creating an elegantly-designed food-cart search app which I like to call cart-blánche.
