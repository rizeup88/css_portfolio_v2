Early Childhood Education Mobile App

iOS/Android Mobile App Prototype

For most parents, there are major milestones they prepare for in advance with their children. One of the biggest, is selecting a daycare or early childhood education center to both educate their children, and care for them while they are at work. Yet, for some centers, a major revelation has taken place in recent years. How can the staff and teachers help parents become more involved with what their children are learning, and bring that education home?

In November 2018, I joined a team of six creative individuals to solve this problem for a reputable early childhood education company. Over the course of three months, we conducted thorough user-research to determine our differentiating features, designed comprehensive wireframes to test with parents, and conducted design reviews with key stakeholders in the company. All of this was performed with attention paid to the company's recognizable branding that conveys security and trust.

After three months consisiting of four design sprints, we completed the app with a fully developed prototype built on a React-based framework. Reactions from parents were predominantly positive, with many parents mentioning this was an app they would enjoy using a lot to make sure their children are receiving quality education. This also served as a unique opportunity for our team to educate the company we worked with on designing in an Agile way.
